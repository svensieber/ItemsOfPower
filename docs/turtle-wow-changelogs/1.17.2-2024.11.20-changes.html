The first batch of changes post 1.17.2 release are out. Mind you that with the changed situation of having the launcher we will do updates way more frequently. We're still incredibly busy getting enough data and making sure every bug that impacts class balance is fixed so we're starting conservatively for most classes.

Please make sure to also visit the November 20 changelog as this contains quite some bug fixes that might alter player / class power.

UPDATES | Dated: November 20 2024


DRUID

Barkskin (Feral) is no longer a magic effect, and only direct physical damage consumes its charges.
Improved Shred Buff duration increased from 5 seconds to 10 seconds.



MAGE

Hot Streak Increased effect duration from 30 seconds to 3 minutes.


HUNTER

We're redistributing power of Hunter's Marksmanship a little as Improved Steady Shot was overvalued and Piercing Shot undervalued. We're also looking into reducing the major power upgrade Survival Hunters got this patch while leveling.

Improved Steady Shot Reduced bonus damage from 13/25% to 8/15%.
Piercing Shot Increased bleed damage from 10/20% to 15/30%.
Carve Cooldown increased from 6 seconds to 10 seconds and added new ranks, where the highest rank has the current damage while earlier ranks deal less damage.

Carve Lower Rank Nerfs:
Rank 1 (Level 20/Talent): 50% Weapon Damage
Rank 2 (Level 38/NEW): 55% Weapon Damage
Rank 3 (Level 56/NEW): 60% Weapon Damage
Mongoose Bite Reduced the weapon damage scaling for earlier ranks. The highest rank will have the same scaling as before.

Mongoose Bite Lower Rank Nerfs:
Rank 1 (Level 16): 45% Weapon Damage + 25 Damage
Rank 2 (Level 30): 50% Weapon Damage + 45 Damage
Rank 3 (Level 44): 55% Weapon Damage + 75 Damage



PALADIN

Paladin is another class where the power we gave them for this patch trivialized leveling and even some end game content for Protection. We're addressing both by tuning down some of these talents & abilities.

Redoubt Reduced block chance bonus from 6/12/18/24/30% to 3/6/9/12/15%.
Righteous Strikes Zeal effect reworked to the following: Zeal increases the amount of damage blocked by 2/4/6/8/10% per stack. Holy Strike effect is unchanged.
Crusader Strike Reduced the weapon damage scaling for earlier ranks. The highest rank will have the same scaling as before.

Crusader Strike Lower Rank Nerfs:
Rank 1 (Level 8): 35% Weapon Damage + 31 Damage
Rank 2 (Level 20): 50% Weapon Damage + 47 Damage
Rank 3 (Level 30): 65% Weapon Damage + 71 Damage
Rank 4 (Level 40): 80% Weapon Damage + 107 Damage
Rank 5 (Level 50): 90% Weapon Damage + 161 Damage
Judgement of Command now uses melee hit.



PRIEST

We overshot Discipline by quite some, both in PvE and PvP. These changes seek to correct this as well as give Shadow some love back.

Mind Flay Spellpower scaling increased from 75% to 90%, meaning every tick gains damage equal to 30% of your spellpower instead of 25%.
Vampiric Embrace Reduced cast time from 2 seconds to 1.5 seconds.
Smite Reduced base damage by 10% for all ranks. This is a return to original Vanilla values.
Searing Light Now has a 3 second cooldown and reduced mana cost reduction from 40% to 20%.
Chastise Reduced disorient duration from 3 seconds to 2 seconds when used on enemies and reduced range from 30 yards to 25 yards.
Discipline Priest Tier 2.5 3 Set Bonus: No longer affects the disorient duration.



SHAMAN

Enhancement DPS is currently doing worse than we'd like it to, therefore we are slowly but surely putting power back in. We have noticed though just like other classes & builds that power we've given overbuffed these builds while leveling, we are addressing this as well.

Stormstrike can now proc weapon enhancement effects.
Lightning Strike Added new ranks, where the highest rank has the current damage while earlier ranks deal less damage.

Lightning Strike Lower Rank Nerfs:
Rank 1 (Level 20/Talent): 20% Weapon Physical + 10% Weapon Nature
Rank 2 (Level 38/NEW): 40% Weapon Physical + 15% Weapon Nature
Rank 3 (Level 56/NEW): 60% Weapon Physical + 20% Weapon Nature



WARLOCK


Searing Pain Increased base damage by 10% for all ranks.
Drain Soul Spellpower scaling increased from 16.67% to 20%, mana costs reduced by 20% for all ranks and damage no longer builds up over the duration and instead deals the same damage for all ticks.

The Drain Soul build-up we added doesn't quite achieve what we wanted it to and thus we are removing it and instead putting a little extra power in the base damage.

Dark Harvest Spellpower scaling increased from 20% to 25%.
Nightfall Now a two pointer talent with a 2/4% chance to proc instead of 1/3/5%.
Imp's Firebolt coefficients have been reduced.



Expect to see another round of changes soon while we're collecting data on class & build performance.
