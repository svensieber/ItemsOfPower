ALL
Physical Damage Reduction from Armor is no longer capped at 75%. The extra reduction gained from Armor beyond this point will be diminished. The updated formula will be shared later.

Developer Comment: Currently some classes and specializations are running into the issue of getting hard-capped by Armor. This severly limits their possible build paths. We intend to remedy this by reducing the extra damage reduction beyond 75% gained from extra Armor but removing the hard cap.

Note that we are very aware of the effects this can have on gameplay if the extension of the formula beyond 75% does not have enough diminishing returns, this is taken into account.
