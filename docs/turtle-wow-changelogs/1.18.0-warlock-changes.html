WARLOCK

Baseline Changes
Warlocks now learn Pet Command: Take Position when they learn Summon Imp. This command allows you to select a location to send your pet to, cancelled by clicking Follow again. Take Position obeys line of sight rules, meaning pets cannot be sent to locations you can’t see.
This spell allows Warlocks to more accurately position their demons, putting them out of danger’s way and adjusting their location behind bosses to avoid pets getting parried.
Drain Soul: Changed large part of the mana cost to be dynamic:
Rank 1 (Level 10): 72 Mana -> 15 Initial + 10 Every Second (75 Total over 6 Seconds)
Rank 2 (Level 24): 188 Mana -> 35 Initial + 25 Every Second (185 Total over 6 Seconds)
Rank 3 (Level 38): 324 Mana -> 70 Initial + 40 Every Second (310 Total over 6 Seconds)
Rank 4 (Level 52): 432 Mana -> 100 Initial + 55 Every Second (430 Total over 6 Seconds)
Rank 5 (Level 60): 524 Mana -> 135 Initial + 65 Every Second (525 Total over 6 Seconds)

Developer Comment: Used for both farming Soul Shards and as a filler ability of Affliction, having such high cost for sometimes only 1 or 2 seconds of uptime was unnecessarily punishing.
Health Funnel and Mana Funnel: Increased range from 20 yards to 30 yards.
Stones - Firestone, Felstone and Wrathstone: Now usable while in combat.
Doomguard: Can no longer use Rend. Now has the ability "Infernal Blade":
"For the next 12 sec, weapon attacks deal 30% additional Fire damage which engulfs the target in flames dealing 250 Fire damage over 5 sec. Stacks up to 3 times."
Spellstone: No longer a passive buff, instead its an active item that when used removes all Magic effects from the casters and grants them a shield that absorbs magic damage equal to the caster's level times 12 for 30 seconds. Shares a cooldown with Healthstones.

Developer Comment: We're returning this signature spell back to its vanilla state with slight adjustments and as a consumable instead of off-hand, adding a strategic decision.
Rain of Fire: Now deals damage every 1 second instead of 2 seconds. Overall damage stays the same.
Soul Fire: Spellpower coefficient reduced from 125% to 114%.

Developer Comment: We're normalizing the scaling to make up for the increase of Improved Soul Fire's power, keeping PvP burst potential in check while helping out PvE Destruction builds.


Affliction
Improved Debilitating Curses: Renamed to "Improved Curse of Weakness" and no longer increases Curse of Exhaustion's slow.
Fel Concentration: Now requires 2 points instead of 5 points and now affects all Affliction spells. This means that it can also prevent pushback while casting Fear and Howl of Terror.
New Talent (Row 3 / 3 Points): Sinister Pursuit
"Increase the movement speed of your demons by 5/10/15% for 3 seconds after they attack a target affected by Affliction effects."
Curse of Exhaustion: Movement speed reduction reduced from 30% to 10%.
New Talent (Row 5 / 2 Points): Improved Curse of Exhaustion
“Increases the speed reduction of your Curse of Exhaustion by 15/30%.”

Developer Comment: Curse of Exhaustion was too easily accessible by other specializations which provided them with oppressive levels of control. We're now making its full power only accessible to Affliction warlocks.


Demonology
Soul Entrapment: Damage increase while you have no pet increased from 1/2/3% to 2/4/6%.
Demonic Sacrifice: Benefits gained from sacrificing demons changed:
Imp: 6% Fire Damage -> 4% Spell Damage
Voidwalker: Unchanged.
Succubus: 6% Shadow Damage -> -10% Threat Generation
Felhunter: 2% Total Mana Regeneration every 4 seconds -> 3% Total Mana Regeneration every 4 seconds
Power Overwhelming: Now removes all crowd control from your demon on activation and costs 40% base health instead of maximum health.

Developer Comment: We're adding versatility and tactical choices to make up for the disadvantages that come with relying on your pet as much as Demonology does. Also the spell will now reward giving demons more stamina rather than punish it.
Demonic Precision: Percentage of spell critical and hit chance transferred reduced from 33/66/100% to 30/60/90% but now grants your demon 1/2/3% spell hit, spell critical chance, physical hit and physical critical chance baseline.

Developer Comment: We're shifting some power to baseline values, so this talent is worthwhile taking for the levelers.
Master Demonologist: Removed secondary effects added to demons previously and removed greater demon specific bonuses, instead all greater demons now gain the same benefit and drawback. The new effects are the following:
Imp: 3/6/9/12/15% reduced spell costs.
Voidwalker: 2/4/6/8/10% reduced physical damage taken.
Succubus: 2/4/6/8/10% increased all damage done.
Felhunter: 0.2/0.4/0.6/0.8/1 increased all resistances per level.
Greater Demons: 2/4/6/8/10% increased spell critical chance and 8/16/24/32/40% reduced healing taken.

Developer Comment: The removed secondary effects failed to sufficiently improve gameplay, sometimes even hindering it. At the same time, we're unifying Greater demon effects, including the Doomguard.


Destruction
Improved Shadow Bolt: Renamed to "Shadow Vulnerability" and can now be applied by Drain Soul by its damage ticks with the same chance as Shadow Bolt.
Destructive Reach: Now also increases the radius of Hellfire.
Improved Soul Fire: Fire damage increase increased from 8% to 10%.
